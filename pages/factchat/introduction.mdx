# FactChat Introduction

## What is FactChat?

FactChat is a generative AI-powered chatbot platform that allows anyone to train AI with documents and build purpose-driven chatbots easily and efficiently.

<Info title="Platform Overview">
FactChat combines state-of-the-art language models with document training capabilities to create specialized AI assistants tailored to your organization's needs.
</Info>

## Key Benefits

### Security-Focused

All data remains stored within internal infrastructure without external transmission, making it ideal for universities and public institutions requiring strict data protection.

### Cost-Efficient

Organizations can avoid expensive cloud AI services by running advanced language models on their own servers, reducing operational expenses.

### User-Friendly Creation

The platform enables rapid chatbot development with the ability to train bots using up to 100 documents for domain-specific accuracy.

### Familiar Interface

FactChat offers ChatGPT-level user experience powered by models like GPT and Claude, making it accessible to broad audiences.

## System Requirements

<Warning title="Minimum Requirements">
Ensure your system meets these requirements for optimal performance:

- **RAM**: Minimum 4GB (8GB recommended)
- **Processor**: Dual-core or higher
- **Internet**: Stable broadband connection
- **Screen resolution**: 1024x768 or higher
</Warning>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up and Login">
    Create your FactChat account and log in to access the platform dashboard. New users receive initial credits to explore all features.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your First Chatbot">
    Navigate to the chatbot creation wizard and select your preferred base model (GPT, Claude, or Gemini). Give your chatbot a descriptive name and purpose.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Train with Documents">
    Upload up to 100 documents to train your chatbot on domain-specific knowledge. Supported formats include PDF, DOCX, TXT, and Markdown.

    <Tip title="Training Best Practices">
    For best results, use well-structured documents with clear headings and consistent formatting. Remove any unnecessary content before uploading.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test and Deploy">
    Test your chatbot with sample queries to verify it responds accurately. Once satisfied, deploy it for your team or organization to use.

    <Success>
    Your chatbot is now live! You can continue to refine it by adding more documents or adjusting its configuration.
    </Success>
  </Step>
</Steps>
