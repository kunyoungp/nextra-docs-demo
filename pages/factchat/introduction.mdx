# FactChat Introduction

## What is FactChat?

FactChat is a generative AI-powered chatbot platform that allows anyone to train AI with documents and build purpose-driven chatbots easily and efficiently.

## Key Benefits

### Security-Focused

All data remains stored within internal infrastructure without external transmission, making it ideal for universities and public institutions requiring strict data protection.

### Cost-Efficient

Organizations can avoid expensive cloud AI services by running advanced language models on their own servers, reducing operational expenses.

### User-Friendly Creation

The platform enables rapid chatbot development with the ability to train bots using up to 100 documents for domain-specific accuracy.

### Familiar Interface

FactChat offers ChatGPT-level user experience powered by models like GPT and Claude, making it accessible to broad audiences.

## System Requirements

- **RAM**: Minimum 4GB (8GB recommended)
- **Processor**: Dual-core or higher
- **Internet**: Stable broadband connection
- **Screen resolution**: 1024x768 or higher

## Getting Started

FactChat provides structured guidance through product introduction, getting-started guides, and feature-specific documentation to help users maximize the platform's capabilities.
